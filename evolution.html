T.V Show Evolution: Classic to Modern TV

The Brady Bunch 1969

The show was conceived by Sherwood Schwartz and revolved around the life of a large complex blended family, which comes together when Mike Brady, the father of three sons, marries Carol Martin, the mother of three girls.

Felicity 1998

The show follows a young freshman college student named Felicity Porter who moves to New York chasing a high-school crush, but ends up discovering herself and making new friends.

Top Chef 2006

is a reality TV competition series where aspiring chefs compete in culinary challenges, with contestants eliminated each week until one remains, ultimately crowned "Top Chef".

Gossip Girl 2007

This show is about a blogger using the pseudonym “Gossip Girl” follows a group of wealthy high schoolers on Manhattan's Upper East Side and exposes the secrets of their private and social lives for all to read.

The Good Fight 2017

It is a legal drama that acts as a spin-off and sequel to "The Good Wife," focusing on Diane Lockhart and her goddaughter Maia Rindell, whose reputations and savings are destroyed by a financial scam, forcing them to join a new, prestigious law firm in Chicago.

Euphoria 2019

The show follows a group of high school students navigating drugs, sex, trauma, identity, and the challenges of adolescence.

Squid Game 2021

It is a South Korean dystopian survival thriller action television series where impoverished players risk their lives to compete in deadly children's games for a large cash prize.

The Recruit 2022

A Netflix spy adventure television series created by Alexi Hawley about Owen Hendricks, a new CIA lawyer, who gets caught up in international conflicts when a former asset threatens to expose the agency, sending him on dangerous missions.
